1 Kondrachov’s theorem

The generalised version of Kondrachov’s theorem is much easier to prove

Theorem 1 (Kondrachov). Theorem None holds when one replaces $\Omega $ by a compact Riemannian manifolds of dimension $n$.

Proof
Cover $M$ by finitely many small geodesic ball $B(Q_ i,\delta )$ subordinating a partition of unity $\sum _{i=1}^ N \chi _ i = 1$, then if a sequence $\{ u_ n\} _ n \subset W^{k,q}$ is bounded then $\{ \chi _ i u_ n\} _ n$ is also bounded in $W^{k,q}$. The conclusion follows using Remark None and the Euclidean version of Kondrachov’s theorem.
