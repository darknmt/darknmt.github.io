\begin{theorem}[Hopf-Rinow]

\label{org68a701f}
Given a Riemannian manifold \(M\), the followings are equivalent
\begin{enumerate}
\item Every geodesic is well-defined at all time
\item Closed and bounded sets are compact.
\end{enumerate}
